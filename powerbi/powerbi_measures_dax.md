# DAX Measures — Sales Analytics Dashboard

This document describes the DAX measures used in the Microsoft Fabric E‑commerce Sales Analytics project.

---

## Revenue Measures

**Total Revenue**  
Total Revenue = SUM(FactSales[revenue])  
Calculates the total revenue generated by all sales transactions.

**Average Revenue per Line**  
Average Revenue per Line = AVERAGE(FactSales[revenue])  
Calculates the average revenue per sales record.

**Average Unit Price**  
Average Unit Price = AVERAGE(FactSales[unit_price])  
Calculates the average price of sold products.

---

## Sales Volume Measures

**Total Quantity**  
Total Quantity = SUM(FactSales[quantity])  
Calculates the total number of units sold.

**Average Quantity per Sale**  
Average Quantity per Sale = AVERAGE(FactSales[quantity])  
Calculates the average quantity per transaction.

---

## Orders Measures

**Total Orders**  
Total Orders = DISTINCTCOUNT(FactSales[order_id])  
Counts the number of unique orders.

**Total Sales Lines**  
Total Sales Lines = COUNTROWS(FactSales)  
Counts the total number of rows in the sales fact table.

---

## Business KPIs

**Average Order Value**  
Average Order Value = DIVIDE([Total Revenue], [Total Orders])  
Calculates the average revenue per order.

**Revenue per Unit**  
Revenue per Unit = DIVIDE([Total Revenue], [Total Quantity])  
Calculates the average revenue generated per unit sold.

**Orders per Day**  
Orders per Day = DIVIDE([Total Orders], DISTINCTCOUNT(DimDate[date_id]))  
Calculates the average number of orders per day.

---

## Time Intelligence Measures

**Revenue MTD (Month‑to‑Date)**  
Revenue MTD = TOTALMTD([Total Revenue], DimDate[date_id])  
Calculates cumulative revenue from the beginning of the current month.

**Revenue YTD (Year‑to‑Date)**  
Revenue YTD = TOTALYTD([Total Revenue], DimDate[date_id])  
Calculates cumulative revenue from the beginning of the current year.

---

## Day‑over‑Day Analysis

**Revenue Previous Day**  
Revenue Prev Day = CALCULATE([Total Revenue], DATEADD(DimDate[date_id], -1, DAY))  
Returns revenue from the previous day.

**Revenue DoD Change**  
Revenue DoD Change = [Total Revenue] - [Revenue Prev Day]  
Calculates the difference in revenue compared to the previous day.

**Revenue DoD %**  
Revenue DoD % = DIVIDE([Revenue DoD Change], [Revenue Prev Day])  
Calculates the percentage change in revenue compared to the previous day.

---

## Channel Analysis

**Revenue Share %**  
Revenue Share % = DIVIDE([Total Revenue], CALCULATE([Total Revenue], ALL(DimChannel)))  
Calculates the revenue contribution of each channel relative to total revenue.
